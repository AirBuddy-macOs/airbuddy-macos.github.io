AirBuddy 2.8.1

Experience AirPods on your Mac like never before. Open the case next to your Mac to see battery status instantly, connect with one click, or swipe to switch listening modes. Custom alerts keep you informed, menu bar shows all nearby devices.

Beautiful UI

Open your AirPods case near your Mac for immediate status. Or trigger with a custom keyboard shortcut to check AirPods or Beats anytime.

Battery Alerts

Get notified when batteries run low or finish charging. Customize thresholds per device—they sync across your Macs.

Menu Bar Overview

Track AirPods, iPhones, Macs, and peripherals from the menu bar icon. See status of nearby Macs running AirBuddy too.

Quick Actions

Global shortcuts to toggle listening modes, show status, connect nearest headset, and more—from anywhere.

Smart Stats & Magic Handoff

View battery usage stats, call and listening times. Hand off Magic Mouse, Keyboard, or Trackpad to another Mac with one click.

How It Works

1. Open Case Nearby

Simply open your AirPods case next to your Mac. Battery status appears instantly, just like on iPhone.

2. Connect & Control

One click connects. Swipe down to switch listening modes while connecting. Use menu bar for overview.

3. Set Alerts & Shortcuts

Customize battery thresholds and notifications. Assign global shortcuts for quick actions from anywhere.

4. Track & Handoff

Monitor stats across devices. Hand off Magic accessories seamlessly between Macs.

FAQ

Is this the full version?

Yes, AirBuddy 2.8.1 is fully activated. No purchase or license required—ready to use immediately.

What devices are supported?

AirPods (all generations including 4th gen and Pro 2nd gen USB-C), AirPods Max (USB-C), Beats, Magic accessories, iPhones, iPads, and more.

Does it work on latest macOS?

Fully compatible with macOS Sequoia, Ventura, Sonoma, and Tahoe betas. Ongoing improvements for future releases.

Can I customize everything?

Yes. Thresholds, shortcuts, alerts, UI styles, and handoff settings—all configurable per device and synced.

How do I get started?

Install, grant Bluetooth permissions, open your case. Customize in Settings. You're set.
